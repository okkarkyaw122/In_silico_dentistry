In silico Dentistry

n silico dentistry uses computer-based modeling and simulations—enhanced by artificial intelligence and machine learning—to enable virtual diagnosis, treatment planning, biomechanical analysis, and predictive modeling without physical or clinical trials.

Simulation Analysis

Simulation analysis in dentistry enhances treatment accuracy, safety, and outcomes by modeling real-time clinical scenarios. It supports diagnostic planning, risk assessment, and patient-specific decision-making through advanced digital tools.
